What is Suburbia Film Festival

Suburbia Film Festival is not just a celebration of film as a medium of artistic expression, but a platform that serves to highlight and cultivate the often overlooked creativity and ambition of a new generation of filmmakers. Our primary goal as an organization is to provide an environment conducive to the free exchange of art and innovation between students of diverse backgrounds.

The creation of Suburbia Student Film Festival started in the summer of 2016. We, a group of five high school students, wanted to initiate a climate of artistic expression in our fairly mundane corner of Suburbia. We floated a few simple ideas of small projects that we could undertake until someone suggested something more bigger. We decided to take the plunge into our most ambitious project yet: creating a film festival that celebrates the work of young artists aspiring to work in the film industry.
